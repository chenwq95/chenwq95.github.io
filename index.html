About me

I am currently an assistant professor of the School of Software Engineering in the Sun Yat-sen University. Previously, I received a doctorate from the Institute of Artificial Intelligence of Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2022. More recently, I receive a master's degree from Beijing Normal University in 2015 and a bachelor's degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2012, respectively.

My research interests include Natural Language Processing (NLP), Causal Inference, and Code Intelligence.

I am looking for self-motivated students. Please drop me an email if you are interested.
